#### Articules

A, An, The

A: Next word sound like consonant (singular)
  a book
  a table
  a man

An: Next word sound like vocal (singular)
  an apple
  an illusion

For Plural use the work: Some
  Some apples
  Some books

The:
'De' Consonant
  The Car (De car)

'Di' Vowel
  The apple (di apple)
